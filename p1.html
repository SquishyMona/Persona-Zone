New to Persona

Shin Megami Tensei: Persona is the first game in the Persona series! Get to the bottom of the demon overrun of your town and put a stop to whoever is behind it in this entry of the Persona series!

In Persona 1, you'll play as the protagonist. You and your friends are summoned by Philemon after playing a game called "Persona". He gives you all the power to summon a Persona, a manifestation of your inner-self...

Soon after this, you go to visit your friend in the hospital, Maki, who has been bed-ridden for a while. During this visit, however, you and your friends find your town is changing and is being overrun by demons! With your Personas, it's up to you to stop them and reclaim your town!

Gameplay

In Persona 1, you'll be traversing your home town in Japan, as while as many other buildings in the area. It's important to be prepared, as encounters with Demons are random and can happen at any moment! Strangthen you and your party members Personas to gain the upper hand in battle, and prepare for battles with other Persona users!
