Dive into the dark tale of Persona 3, the first entry in the Persona series to introduce so many features that are now key elements of the modern Persona games today, like the "One More!" mechanic, social links, the calander system, and more!

In Persona 3, you'll play as the protagonist, who has returned to his hometown of Tatsumi Port Island, about 10 years after his parents passed away. Your journey back home seems to be normal, but when you finally arrive, something seems to be off...

When making your way to your new dormatory, you see the jarring scenary around you: the moon glows green, coffins litter the streets, and all humans seem to have vanished. When you arrive at the dorms, you encounter your new dorm mates, who explain that you are in the phenomenon known as the Dark Hour, a time between one day and the next, where Shadows roam and attack!

Gameplay

Persona 3 is all about climbing the mysterious tower
called "Tartarus", a tower that only appears during the
Dark Hour. Shadows live here and will attack you, and
you and your team have to figure out the best way to
get rid of them! Defeating Shadows is the main objective,
so do your best to gain the upper hand in battle and make
quick work of them!

Persona 3 is the first game in the series to intoduce the "One More!" mechanic. In battle, when you hit an enemies weak point, they'll become downed, and you'll be able to take another action! The more weak points you hit, the more actions you'll be able to take!

One of the key elements of the modern Persona games are social links, and Persona 3 is the first game to feature them! By talking to and hanging out with various different characters in the game, you'll level up your social links and gain extra benefits when fusing Personas!
