Experience an incredible story, stunning artstyle, and smooth gameplay in the latest entry in the Persona series, Persona 5! Released in 2016, this game shook the world of JRPGs, and put the Persona series in the spotlight for the very first time!

In Persona 5, you'll play as the protagonist. He has moved to Tokyo from his hometown after being accused and charged with assault, a crime which he didn't commit. He has one job: live an honest and normal life until his 1 year probation period is over. However, this will prove to be much more difficult then he may have thought...

Shortly after coming to Tokyo, you accidentaly enter the Metaverse, a world where people's deepest and most warped desires take form. Here, the protagonist awakens to his Persona, a manifestation of someone's inner rebellion. With this newfound power, you decide to form a group, known as the Phantom Theives, to reform society and make all the rotton criminals confess their crimes!

Gameplay

In Persona 5, you and the rest of the Phantom Theives traverse through "Palaces", spaces in the Metaverse that are ruled by a single Palace owner. Inside these Palaces, Shadows roam and will attack you and your group. It's up to you guys to strike back and defeat these monsters, while also maintaining the style and class that the Phantom Theives are known for!

Instead of defeating Shadows, try negotiating with them instead! When you've downed all the enemies, you'll enter a "Hold Up!", where you can choose to talk to the enemy and ask them for money and items in exchange for letting them go free. Play your cards right, and you may even be able to recruit them to your team as a Persona!
