Experience an incredible story, stunning
artstyle, and smooth gameplay in the latest
entry in the Persona series, Persona 5!
Released in 2016, this game
put the Persona series in the
spotlight for the very first time!

In Persona 5, you'll play as the protagonist. He
has moved to Tokyo from his hometown after
being accused and charged with assault, a crime
which he didn't commit. He has one job: live an
honest and normal life until his 1 year
probation period is over...

Shortly after coming to Tokyo, you accidentaly enter
the Metaverse, a world where people's deepest and
most warped desires take form. Here, the
protagonist awakens to his Persona, a manifestation
of someone's inner rebellion. With this newfound
power, you decide to form a group, known as
the Phantom Theives, to reform society and make all
the rotton criminals confess their crimes!

Gameplay

In Persona 5, you and the rest of the Phantom Theives
traverse through "Palaces", spaces in the Metaverse that
are ruled by a single Palace owner. Inside these Palaces,
Shadows roam and will attack you and your group. It's
up to you guys to strike back and defeat these monsters,
while also maintaining the style and class that the
Phantom Theives are known for!

Instead of defeating Shadows, try negotiating with
them instead! When you've downed all the enemies,
you'll enter a "Hold Up!", where you can choose
to talk to the enemy and ask them for money and
items in exchange for letting them go free. Play
your cards right, and you may even be able to
recruit them to your team as a Persona!

The Phantom Thieves perfer to sneak around
and maintain stealth during their infiltrations.
In Persona 5, you can easily slip into and out
of cover and stay completely hidden to the
enemy. Plan your actions correctly, and sneak up
on and ambush your enemies to gain the upper
hand in battle!

More Persona 5

Persona 5 has had various spin-offs released for it
that expand the story of the game and let fans enjoy
the amazing cast of characters of the game even more,
with some games even introducing new members to the
Phantom Thieves! Can't get enough of Persona 5? These
games are for you!
