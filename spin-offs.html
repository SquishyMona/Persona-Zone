Looking for more Persona?

There have been many Persona spin-offs released throughout the years, many of which that expand the
story of the main games they're based off of! Whether you're looking for a more laid-back rhythm game,
or maybe a more fast-paced, action packed Musou game experience, there's plenty of Persona spin-offs for you!

Make your way through various
labyrinths and dungeons in Persona Q!

Persona Q and Persona Q2 are both dungeon-crawling JRPGS, taking place
in the middle of the various Persona games! The casts of Persona 3, 4
and 5 are brought together and trapped inside a mysterious world full
of Shadows, and they all have to cooperate to find out what's going on
and how to escape! The Persona Q games are great games for long time
Persona fans who want to see all their favorite characters fight and
interact together!

Get groovy and dance off with
your friends in the Persona Dancing series!

The Persona Dancing games are great titles for Persona fans who absolutely
love the music of the series! Each game is a rhythm game featuring music
from the game its based on, as well as fun visuals, and the ability to
dress your favorite characters up in all sorts of different costumes!
Each game features it's own small story mode as well, so this is a great
game for fans who want to take a break from battle and have some fun dancing
away to the incredible music of the Persona series!

Travel across Japan and steal
hearts once again in Persona 5 Strikers!

Taking place directly after the events of Persona 5, dive into
a new story where you'll travel across Japan and change the hearts
of many more criminals across the country! Infiltrate the many Jails
that have popped up in the Metaverse, and experience the action-packed
hack-and-slash gameplay that still retains the classic Persona charm
in this critically acclaimed sequal to Persona 5!
