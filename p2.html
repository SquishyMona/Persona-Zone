Enter Sumaru City, a city located in Japan, and join the cast of Persona 2 on their two-part journey to solve the mystery of rumors suddenly starting to come true in this classic entry in the Persona series!

In the first part, you'll play as Tatsuya, the protagonist. You and your friends are trying to solve the murder mystery of the "Joker Game". If you call your own phone number, a being known as the Joker will be summoned, and if you don't tell him your most precious wish, he erases you from existence!

In the second part, you'll play as Maya, who is trying to find Tatsuya after the events of the first part. You learn that an entity known as Nyatholatep is trying to bring about the end of the world by reuniting all Persona users!

Gameplay

In Persona 2, you'll be traversing Sumaru City in Japan.
Shadows roam everywhere, and you'll randomly encounter
them along your journey, so it's important to be prepared
for anything! Battles will have you selecting your moves
in advance and watching how things play out, so it's
important to make sure that you think carefully and straegize
properly before you decide your next move!

In Persona 2, battles are a bit different than the rest of the Persona games. You'll pick which action you want each character to take first, then you'll watch as the battle commences and the enemy takes their actions. This means that you'll have to plan ahead and think carefully to make sure the battle wages in your favor!

Fusion spells make their first appearance in Persona 2, and are very critical in winning battles! These are special attacks that are performed by 2 or more party members combining their skills for a very strong attack! Make sure you use them whenever you have an opportunity!
