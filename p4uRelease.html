Persona 4 Arena Ultimax is coming
to modern platforms!

Persona 4 Arena Ultimax is back, and this time, you'll be able to play
on the go on the Nintendo Switch, sitting at home with PlayStation 4

release, never before released on consoles! This truly is the definitive edition
of Persona 4 Arena Ultimax, and you can experience it yourself on March 17th, 2022!
