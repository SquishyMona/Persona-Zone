Welcome to Persona Zone!

Welcome to Persona Zone, a website about anything and everything Persona! Scroll down this page to see
all the different Persona games and what they have to offer. Find something you might like? Click on
it to learn even more about it!

Steal the hearts of evil doers
and reform society with The Phantom Thieves

Join the Phantom Thieves as they infiltrate the Metaverse,
a world where people's deepest and darkest desires take form, and
change the hearts of criminals all over Tokyo to make them confess
their crimes, while also living the life of a high school student
with a criminal record in this critically acclaimed JRPG.

Join The Investigation Team as
they solve the mystery of the fog

Experience the dark story of a series of mysterious murders
in the small town of Inaba, and traverse the TV world
to get to the bottom of who's behind these strange homocides,
while also enjoying spending time with your friends
in this light-hearted, but also dark entry in the Persona series

Traverse Tartarus and solve the mystery
of the Dark Hour with SEES

Make friends, go to school, and live out your life as a
high school student during the day, and defeat shadows
during the Dark Hour, the "hidden hour" between one day and
the next, where monsters come out and normal humans turn
into coffins, and investigate what the Dark Hour is, and
why it exists in this deep, dark, and intense Persona story.

Solve the mystery of the Joker and
the Persona Game with Tatsuya and Maya

Rumors have started becoming reality in your home city, and
it all comes back to one person; the Joker, a mysterious
person who, when summoned, forces people to tell them their wishes.
If they refuse, they are turned into emotion-less shells, unable
to do anything. You and your team are determined to get to the
bottom of this, and defeat the Joker once and for all!

End the demon overrun of
your town in the original Persona

One day, you and your friends decide to play the Persona Game,
a ritual type game similar to "Bloody Mary". While playing, you
and your friends fall unconcious and find yourselves in a strange
world. You are greeted by a man named Philemon, who grants you all
the power to summon Personas, a manefestation of your inner
personalities. Shortly after this, demons start appearing in your town,
and its up to you to stop them!
