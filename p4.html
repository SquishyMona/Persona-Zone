Welcome to Persona 4, a game
all about strengthening your friendships
and solving the murder mystery in the
small town of Inaba! Released in 2009
on the PS2, this game was a new, more
lighthearted take on the Persona series!

In Persona 4, you'll play as the protagonist,
who has gone to live with his Uncle in a town
called Inaba, for the year while his parents
are away on a trip. While trying to adjust
to your new life with some new friends, you
learn of a series of strange murders that have
been occuring in the town...

Soon after this, you discover that the culprit
has been throwing their victims into TV's, where
they then enter the TV World and get killed by
their Shadows. You and your friends find out
that you too can enter the TV World, and you
can use the power of Persona to defeat these
Shadows, protect the people who have been
thrown in, and get to the bottom of this
murder mystery!

Gameplay

Persona 4 is all about traversing through dungeons and
defeating the Shadows within in order to reach the
victims Shadow self, so that you can defeat it and
save the victim from their Shadow. The key to making
it through each dungeon is sneaking up on Shadows to
gain the advantage in battle, exposing your enemies
weak points, and leveling up to defeat stronger enemies!

Shadows will be roaming throughout
every dungeon and will attack you
immediatly after they notice you. It's
important to be sneaky when you see
one. Walk up behind a Shadow and attack
them without them noticing, and you'll
gain the advantage in battle!
