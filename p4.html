Welcome to Persona 4, a game
all about strengthening your friendships
and solving the murder mystery in the
small town of Inaba! Released in 2009
on the PS2, this game was a new, more
lighthearted take on the Persona series!

In Persona 4, you'll play as the protagonist,
who has gone to live with his Uncle in a town
called Inaba, for the year while his parents
are away on a trip. While trying to adjust
to your new life with some new friends, you
learn of a series of strange murders that have
been occuring in the town...
